Our Services.

What we offer

Online Counseling

Through Silver Lining, users can connect with licensed therapists and counselors via secure and confidential online sessions. This service offers a convenient and flexible way for individuals to discuss their concerns, feelings, and challenges with a qualified professional who can provide guidance and support.


24/7 Crisis Helpline:

Silver Lining offers a confidential crisis helpline accessible 24/7. Trained crisis counselors are available to provide immediate support and intervention to individuals experiencing acute distress or emotional crisis.

Support Groups

Silver Lining facilitates online support groups where individuals with similar experiences can come together, share their stories, and provide mutual support. These groups foster a sense of community and understanding, creating a safe for users to feel heard and validated.


Educational Resources for Families and Caregivers:

We offer resources specifically tailored to families and caregivers of individuals with mental health challenges. These materials aim to foster understanding, empathy, and effective support for their loved ones.
